We are a cooperative cycling center dedicated to making the use of bicycles as transportation and fun possible for all members of the Bozeman and greater Gallatin Valley communities.

  • Get a bike

    We have a number of programs to provide bicycles to all types of people. We offer free bicycles, bicycles in exchange for volunteering, and bicycles for purchase.

  • Fix your bike

    We will help you fix your bike! For a nominal fee you can set up at one of our work stations and use our tools to fix your bike.

  • Get parts

    Used parts are available at very reasonable prices. If you cannot afford to pay we will still find a way to get you back on the road - no one turned away due to lack of funds!

  • Volunteer

    You don't need any bicycle repair knowledge or skills whatsoever to help out at the Kitchen. You can even count this towards your community service!
