donate

We are a non-profit 501(c)(3) organization, funded by donations and sales of items we refurbish with volunteered time.

In order for us to offer the level of quality service and time that this community needs we request your help. This can be in the form of a monetary donation, volunteer time, or a gift of an item we might need. If you'd like to make a monetary donation we accept cash (in person), check (in person or via mail), or through Paypal.

Amazon gives us profits from your shopping

You may also shop on Amazon via their AmazonSmile program. By making us your choice organization, we recieve a portion of any purchase you make. It's pretty easy! Learn more on on AmazonSmile's about page.
